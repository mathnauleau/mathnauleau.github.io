Overview

Our team has already launched two apps, the "Gall & Gall App", and the "Mijn Etos App". As the principal UX designer and "system guardian", my role ensures consistency and a unified User Experience across these apps.

What is a White Label product?

White labeling is the process of rebranding a single product (or service) for multiple companies to sell as their own. With this, each company can focus on their business and marketing effort without worrying on the technical aspect

For this to work, White Label product need a design system to ensure consistency and eliminates redundancy across various pages and channels in a standardized approach.

White-label design is a popular strategy for building SaaS products. Instead of manually changing the same element in multiple files (also prone to human error), a design system allows for automatic updates throughout the application by just changing a single instance. Based on the existing workflow, create a flexible white-label product using a white-label design system that is brandable, accessible, and scalable.
